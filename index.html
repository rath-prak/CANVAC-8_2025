Indications

For an active prophylactic immunization of dogs from 6 weeks of age against distemper, hepatitis, laryngotracheitis, parvovirosis, parainfluenza and leptospirosis (3 strains).

Contraindications

Do not use in cases of acute infectious disease, an organ insufficiency, fever, parasitic infestations, high sensitivity to any vaccine component, in a high level of pregnancy and just after parturition.

Dosage and method of administration

The immunization dose per animal is 1 ml, irrespective of species, age, sex and weight. The vaccine should be administered subcutaneously (s.c.).

Primary vaccination

Primary vaccination is carried out in dogs from age 6–8 weeks, second dose is administered after 3 weeks. It is advised to administer third dose after 3–4 weeks after second dose in youngsters with specific colostral antibodies and youngsters, to whom was administered second dose before finished 12th week of age.

Revaccination

To maintain persistent immunity, regular annual revaccination is advisable. When using the vaccine CANVAC R as a solvent, use of this combination undergoes the same processes.

Size of package

3 × 1 dose, 6 × 1 dose, 30 × 1 dose, 60 × 1 dose. Each dose represents the combination of one vial with lyophilized part and one vial with solvent.
